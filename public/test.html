Development Roadmap

Solana mainnet

Deploy the project on the Solana mainnet and launch our own token through the Pump platform.

Promotion

Launch the project on popular blockchains like Neo and Ethereum to enhance its visibility and promotion.

NFT privatization

Privatize 3D data NFTs to ensure they are visible only to the owner, enhancing their utility.

NFT Leasing

On the basis of NFT privatization, we add value through NFT leasing, allowing only the owner to print the 3D data.
